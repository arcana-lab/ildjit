JITBOOLEAN IRDATA_isAMethodID ( ir_item_t *  item)

Check if a IR variable or constant is an identificator of a method.

Check if a IR variable or a IR constant includes the identificator of a method.

Notice that the identificator of a method and its entry point (the one checked by IRDATA_isAFunctionPointer) are different.

In order to get the IR method from the method ID, you can:

2 ir_item_t *irItem;

8 if (IRDATA_isAMethodID(irItem)){

17 }
